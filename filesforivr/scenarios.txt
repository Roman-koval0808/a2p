CLEARSKY A2P — CALL SCENARIOS

Setup: Customer calls our business number and hears the menu:
Press 1 = Billing, 2 = Sales, 3 = Support.


SCENARIO 1 — BILLING (Press 1)
Customer presses 1 and leaves a voicemail asking for their balance.
The system looks up their balance ($1,130) and drafts a reply text.
You approve it, and the text sends.


SCENARIO 2 — SALES (Press 2)
A new customer presses 2 and leaves a voicemail asking to book July 1 at 2pm.
The system creates their profile, flags it as a sales opportunity, checks the
time is open, and drafts a confirmation text. You approve it, and the text sends.


SCENARIO 3 — DROP CALL (no input)
Customer hangs up without pressing anything.
The system logs it as a dropped call (time, date, number). Nothing else.


Every call shows up in one communication log, with a summary, the digit they
pressed, and whether they left a message. Nothing goes out without your approval.
