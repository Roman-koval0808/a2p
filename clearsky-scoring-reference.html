🔄 Unidirectional Progression Flow
The ClearSky Lead Intelligence scoring pipeline operates in a strict, unidirectional sequence:
  • Score ALWAYS Drives the Bucket: Points accumulated from page interactions, scrolls, clicks, and incoming calls push the lead's live score higher.
  • Bucket NEVER Changes the Score: Moving to a higher intent bucket is a result of score increases or specific active signals. Promoted buckets do not retroactively modify the underlying engagement score.
  • Sequence Reveals Intent: Individual events have a base score, but sequential paths unlock multipliers. It is the sequence itself that reveals deep buyer intent that independent events cannot.
Emergency
Fires immediately on any single urgency signal with no score threshold. Overrides everything and never demotes within the session.
Trigger: URL pattern (/burst-pipe), call transcription keywords, or clicking emergency Call CTA.
Active Project
Indicates a customer actively seeking services. Requires:
  • Score $\ge 45$ AND at least one conversion event.
  • OR at least two qualifying active signals (e.g. Hero CTA click + focusing phone field).
Comparison
Indicates a customer comparing options. Requires:
  • Score $\ge 25$ OR visiting reviews/pricing pages.
  • OR at least two qualifying comparison signals.
Research
Initial browsing behavior. Triggered by **any trackable signal** (score $\ge 9$, page view, scroll, or click).
📐 Base Points vs. Multipliers (Two-Tier Structure)
A single standalone event yields its base points. When executed in sequence, specific multiplier rules apply to reveal true customer focus:
1. Standalone Event
A standalone review_read event.
Base delta = +4 points
2. Post-Gallery Review Path
Reading reviews after dragging the before/after slider (ba_slider_drag).
Base +4 × 2.5 Multiplier = +10 points
3. Comparison Finalizer Path
Completing the third step in the comparison confirmation flow.
Base +4 × 3.0 Multiplier = +12 points
⏳ Exponential Live Decay
Between sessions, score_live decays exponentially after the bucket's grace period expires. However, score_raw represents the lifetime historical engagement and never decreases.
Emergency half-life: 2 days (Grace: 1 day)
Active half-life: 14 days (Grace: 3 days)
Comparison half-life: 30 days (Grace: 7 days)
Research half-life: 60 days (Grace: 14 days)
🛑 Demotion Rules
Demotion is never triggered by idle time alone. For a lead to be demoted to a lower bucket, **both of the following conditions must be true simultaneously**:
  1. The bucket-specific grace period has expired.
  2. The live decayed score (score_live) falls below the threshold:
    • Active demotes to Comparison if score < 35
    • Comparison demotes to Research if score < 20
    • Research demotes to Unclassified if score < 8
